The way individual investors access professional trading strategies has evolved significantly. Qubit Alpha represents the next leap forward in this evolution.

## 1.0: Quantitative Funds (The Black Box)

Historically, top-tier trading strategies were the exclusive domain of institutions and the ultra-wealthy.

* **High Barrier**: Minimum investments often starting at $1M+.
* **Black Box**: Investors have zero visibility into specific trades or logic.
* **Uncertain Quality**: Returns depend entirely on luck and the fund's marketing ability.
* **Lock-up Periods**: Capital is tied up for months or years with limited liquidity.

## 2.0: CEX Copy Trading (The Wild West)

Centralized Exchanges (CEX) democratized access, but introduced critical risks.

* **Custodial Risk**: *"Not your keys, not your coins."*
* **Limited Access**: Only traders who onboard with the specific exchange are available.
* **Lack of Top Strategies**: Top-tier traders rarely open their positions for copying.
* **Opaque Data**: Performance metrics can be cherry-picked.
