Learn how to manage your funds on Qubit Alpha.

## Asset Management

Qubit Alpha provides flexible options for managing your funds depending on your account type.

### 1. Managed Accounts

*Accounts created directly within Qubit Alpha.*

* **Operation**: You can Deposit and Withdraw funds directly through the **Qubit Alpha Interface**.
* **Convenience**: Seamlessly integrated for the best user experience.

### 2. Self-Managed Accounts

*Includes API Wallets and imported Hyperliquid Sub-accounts.*

* **Operation**: Please manage your funds (Deposit/Withdraw) directly on the [Hyperliquid](https://hyperliquid.xyz) platform.
* **Control**: You retain full operational control over these accounts. Qubit Alpha connects only for trading execution.

## Fees

* **Qubit Alpha Fees**: **None**. Qubit Alpha does not charge any fees for deposits, withdrawals, or transfers.
* **Network Fees**: Standard gas fees (Arbitrum) or bridge fees imposed by the Hyperliquid network still apply.
