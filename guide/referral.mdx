The Qubit Alpha Referral System enables you to earn rewards by inviting other traders. It features a transparent, multi-tier structure offering both direct (L1) and indirect (L2) commissions. Commissions are settled **every hour**.

## Referral Tiers & Rewards

| Tier | Min. Fee Referred | L1 Reward | L2 Reward | Discount | Invites |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Normal** | $0 | **30%** | **2%** | **2%** | 5 |
| **Advanced** | ≥ $10 | **35%** | **3%** | **3%** | 20 |
| **VIP** | ≥ $500 | **40%** | **5%** | **5%** | **Unlimited** |

## How Referee Discounts Work

When a new user signs up using your referral code, they receive a **trading fee discount** for their first **30 days**.

* Discounts are directly applied to trading fees on-chain automatically.
* Referees do not need to manually claim any discount rebates.

## Referral Attribution

Qubit Alpha follows a **First-Touch Attribution** policy:

* A user's referrer is determined by the **first referral link** they click.
* Once bound, the referrer code is locked in permanently.
* No overwriting even if the user clicks a different referral link later.

## Earnings & Settlement

### Hourly Settlement

Referral commissions are calculated and settled **every hour**.

### Withdrawals

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Referral Dashboard">
    Go to your Referral Dashboard in the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Balance">
    Ensure your available balance is at least **$1** (minimum withdrawal).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Withdrawal">
    Submit a withdrawal request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Processing">
    Once approved, funds are batch-transferred directly to your on-chain wallet via Hyperliquid.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Anti-Fraud & Eligibility

* Self-referrals are strictly prohibited and will be detected.
* Standard anti-sybil checks apply to validate invited users.
* Accounts violating referral rules may have rewards forfeited.
