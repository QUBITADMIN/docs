## Platform Fees

Qubit Alpha charges **no fees** for deposits, withdrawals, or internal transfers.

The standard platform trading fee is **5.5 bps** per trade, used as the basis for referral commission calculations.

## Network Fees

* **Arbitrum Gas Fees**: Standard gas fees apply for on-chain transactions.
* **Bridge Fees**: Hyperliquid network bridge fees may apply when moving funds.

## Referral Fee Sharing

| Tier | L1 Commission | L2 Commission |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Normal | 30% | 2% |
| Advanced | 35% | 3% |
| VIP | 40% | 5% |

<Note>
Qubit Alpha reserves the right to adjust fee structures. Any changes will be announced via our official channels.
</Note>
