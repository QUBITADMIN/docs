Qubit Alpha maintains a simple and transparent fee structure. We only charge a small fee on your trades to sustain the platform.

## Fee Summary

### Platform Fee

Qubit Alpha charges a flat **0.055% (5.5 bps)** on transaction volume.

### Detailed Breakdown

| Fee Type | Rate | When Applied |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Qubit Alpha Fee | 0.055% | Per trade executed |
| Performance Fee | 0% | No profit sharing |
| Management Fee | 0% | No hidden costs |
| Withdrawal Fee | 0% | Network gas only |

## No Performance Fees

Unlike other platforms, Qubit Alpha does not charge any performance fees. You keep **100% of your trading profits**. The only cost is the nominal 0.055% fee per transaction.

## No Hidden Fees

Qubit Alpha does not charge:

* ❌ Account opening fees
* ❌ Monthly subscription fees
* ❌ Deposit fees
* ❌ Inactivity fees

<Tip>
Always check a curator's fee percentage before starting copy trading. Higher fees require proportionally higher returns to be worthwhile.
</Tip>
