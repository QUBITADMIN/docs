Qubit Alpha ("Qubit Alpha," "we," "our," or "us") provides a non-custodial interface for discovering, monitoring, and copying trades on Hyperliquid and other third-party protocols. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information in connection with our Services.

By using the Services, you agree to our collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information Collection

### A. Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: If you choose to provide it, we may collect your email address, nickname, and other profile details.
- **Correspondence and Content**: When you contact us, we may collect your name, contact information, and the content of any communication.
- **Referral or Invite Codes**: If you enter an invite/referral code, we may store it to apply benefits and for fraud prevention.

### B. Information Collected Automatically

- **Wallet Address and Public On-Chain Data**: We collect the wallet address you use to connect to the Services.
- **Device and Log Information**: We collect data about the device used to access the Services and basic logs to optimize and troubleshoot.
- **Usage Information**: We may collect information about how you interact with the Services to continuously improve the user experience.

## 2. Use of Information

We may collect and use information for the following purposes:

- **Operating and Managing Services**: To provide access, respond to inquiries, enforce our Terms, detect fraud, and comply with legal obligations.
- **Providing Core Features**: To enable wallet-based authentication, trader discovery, copy-trading workflows, and performance analytics.
- **Improving Services**: To analyze user behavior and improve the user experience.
- **Security and Compliance**: To maintain security and comply with applicable laws.

## 3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

- **Service Providers**: We may share information with third-party providers for security, analytics, and blockchain transaction monitoring.
- **Affiliates and Advisors**: We may share information with affiliates and professional advisors for audits and compliance.
